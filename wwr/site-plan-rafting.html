Overview

Purpose

Our online presence will allow us to share our passion for adventure and meet new travel companions, provide them with information about our services and tours, enable them to schedule an appointment to travel with us, read safety tips as well as tips to enjoy the trip more, hear experiences and see photos of everything they can share in our adventures.

Audience

Our target customers are groups of people from 8 years old and up, with budgets that are not too expensive, ranging from a moderately low budget for the 1-day single tour packages to a high budget for the multi-day packages, they will be looking to indulge their passion for outdoor adventures and exciting activities, or they may want to learn how to enjoy them safely, when they comes to our website will look for information such as travel maps, experiences of previous travelers, schedules, and activity planning information. They will want to have the opportunity to ask and clarify their specific questions through direct contact or FAQ sections, and they will also want to have the chance to schedule from the site, usually the access to our website will be from a desktop or laptop computer to review all the information clearly from home, occasionally they may access it from a mobile device to show the site to family and friends or share it via social networks.
